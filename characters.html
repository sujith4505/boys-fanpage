Meet the main members of The Boys and The Seven, each with their own motives, powers, and secrets.

  • Billy Butcher

    Role: Leader of The Boys
    Description: Ruthless and cunning, Butcher is driven by a personal vendetta against the Supes—especially Homelander.

  • Hughie Campbell

    Role: Member of The Boys
    Description: Once an ordinary guy, Hughie is the moral core of the group after tragedy strikes his life.

  • Homelander

    Role: Leader of The Seven
    Description: Charismatic yet sociopathic, Homelander is the most powerful Supe—and also the most dangerous.

  • Starlight (Annie January)

    Role: Member of The Seven
    Description: Starlight is a beacon of hope and conscience within the corrupt world of superheroes.

  • Frenchie (Serge)

    Role: Weapons Specialist
    Description: Brilliant and resourceful, Frenchie is fiercely loyal to his chosen family, The Boys.

  • Mother's Milk (MM)

    Role: Second-in-Command
    Description: The group's steady hand, MM is methodical, level-headed, and deeply committed to justice.

  • Queen Maeve (Maggie Shaw)

    Role: Member of The Seven
    Description: Jaded but brave, Maeve struggles to do the right thing in a corrupt system.

  • A-Train (Reggie Franklin)

    Role: The Fastest Man Alive
    Description: Super-speedster A-Train is ambitious and reckless, always looking to be on top—even at a cost.
